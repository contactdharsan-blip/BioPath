# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## Overview

BioPath ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data**: Server logs including access times and error information

### Information You Provide

- **Analysis Queries**: Chemical compounds you search for analysis
- **User Preferences**: Theme settings, interface preferences (stored locally in your browser)

**Important**: We do NOT store your chemical analysis results or queries on our servers. All analysis data remains on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

- Improving and optimizing our service
- Diagnosing technical issues
- Analyzing usage patterns and trends
- Ensuring security and preventing abuse
- Complying with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- Analysis data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
- User preferences are stored locally in your browser using browser storage
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Server logs are retained for security and debugging purposes only

## Third-Party Services

BioPath uses the following external APIs for chemical data:

- **PubChem** (NCBI): Chemical compound data
- **ChEMBL** (EBI): Drug-target interaction data
- **Reactome**: Pathway and biological process data
- **Open Targets**: Drug-target relationship data

These services have their own privacy policies. Please review them:
- [PubChem Privacy](https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/privacy)
- [ChEMBL Terms](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/about/terms-of-use)

## GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the EU:
- You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
- We process data based on legitimate interest and service provision
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing
